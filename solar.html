Tufts Solar Installation

I was a part of the Tufts Energy Group and I led a project to make solar more accessible to students. In the summer of 2017, I took over as the lead of the project. We worked with a local solar installer, Tufts facilities staff, Tufts administration members, and most importantly Tufts students to help guide the direction of this project. Construction completed in 2019 and the system as shown is fully installed and awaiting electrical inspection in order for students to engage with and learn about solar technology.
